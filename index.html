Where Does the Growth Come From?

There are many ways to put your money to work. Here are some common sources of passive returns — no expertise required.

🏦
High-Yield Savings
~2 – 4% per year

Your bank pays you interest just for keeping your money there. Zero risk, completely passive.

🪙
Government Bonds
~3 – 5% per year

Lend money to a government and receive regular interest. Very low risk, widely considered the safest.

📈
Index Funds
~7 – 10% per year

Own a slice of the entire stock market. Historically ~8% annually over decades.

💰
Dividend Stocks
~3 – 6% per year

Companies share profits as dividends. Reinvesting them accelerates compounding.

🏠
REITs (Real Estate)
~4 – 8% per year

Invest in real estate without buying property. REITs pay out rental income as dividends.

Small amounts, consistently saved and compounded, can change your future. The best time to start was yesterday — the second best time is today.
